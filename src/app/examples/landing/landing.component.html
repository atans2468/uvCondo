In January, 2010, the co-founders sat on a couch at David Baker’s home and talked about a hospital and integrated community supported by the Unions in Las Vegas. By August, 2010, Union Village LLC was formed and was circulating its first offering to raise capital. Fast-forward four years to October, 2014 when Henderson Hospital broke ground, and Union Village had signed LOI's for a senior living facility, apartments, a wellness clinic, and retail development. Today, Union Village stands tall as the largest integrated health village in the world and is supported by a fantastic team led by the three partners above.
